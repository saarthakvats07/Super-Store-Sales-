# Super-Store-Sales-
Power BI Dashboard
1.Project Objective
. To analyze retail sales data and identify key business insights.
. To track sales performance, profit trends, and customer segments.
. To help businesses make data-driven decisions using visual analytics.

2.Tools & Technologies
. Power BI
. Data Visualization
. Data Cleaning & Transformation

3.Key Features
. Interactive dashboard showing sales, profit, quantity, and delivery metrics.
. Analysis of sales by category, sub-category, region, and segment.
. Visualization of monthly and yearly sales trends.
. Sales forecasting to predict future performance.

4.Insights
. Identified top-performing product categories and regions.
. Analyzed customer segments and payment modes contributing to revenue.
. Helped understand profit trends and sales growth patterns.
